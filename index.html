SOLAR ENERGY PLAN

Solar Energy Coverage

Portion of monthly energy consumptions planned to be covered by solar energy. Adding battery storage is advisable for coverage above 40%, but this will largely depend on your solar panel provider.

Battery Storage
off/on
Battery storage might significantly increase investment costs and return period in return of higher energy coverage capability, which further reduce carbon emission. For solar energy coverage of above 40%, it is advisable to add energy storage.
PV System Cost

PV System Lifetime

Typical solar panel lifespan is 20-30 years, with an average of 25 years.[2]
Performance Ratio

Coefficient for system losses ranges between 0.5-0.9, with a default value of 0.75. Examples of performance losses in real-world applications are from shadings, inverters, cables, and system temperature.[3]

About

This PWA (Progressive Web App) was made by a master graduate in sustainable buildings to help estimate investments on solar panel installation. Along with the capital and operational costs and savings, this tool also emphasizes the prevention of carbon emission that comes with utilizing solar energy. The overall goal is to support the continuously increasing adoption rate of solar panels towards a global clean energy future.
